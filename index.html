Who Am I?

Just a little bit about me. I am a student at the University of Nevada, Reno studying Computer Science and Engineering. I expect to graduate with a Bachelor of Science in May 2020 with a minor in Mathematics. I’ve always known I wanted to work with technology. My mom often reminds of when I was a child. All I would want for my birthday would be a simple alarm clock or a vacuum, something to play around with. I would sit there for hours just messing around with these things to figure out how they really worked. Once I was old enough to have access to a computer, I really found my passion. I was always on it learning the basics of the hardware and learning what a computer could do. I was first introduced to programming in middle school taking a web design class. At first, I was discouraged and uninterested overwhelmed by what was at my fingertips. I struggled to be creative to really build a nice website from pure html, that’s all they taught us in class, but I knew I found my passion and continued down that road as soon as I got to College.
