School Projects

Foxy Boys & Shape Quest created by: Caleb Gesick, Jared Manning, Daniel Thomas, and Christine Vaughan

These school projects were created using Unity and simple C# scripts for CS328 - Game Design.


Food Fight consists of spinning walls and other obstacles that encourage the players to run around and use them to their advantage. The players shoot different kinds of food, in this case player one shoots bacon and player two shoots pumpkin pie. The goal is to hit your opponent twenty times. The Health is shown at the top and whoever gets their opponent down to zero first wins. Player one, right, uses the arrow keys for movement and the right control button to shoot. Whereas player two, left, uses the standard WASD keys to move and F to shoot.

Foxy Boys is a collaboration game centered around the mechanic of creating and destroying blocks to progress through the levels. The light-colored fox (Builder) can create blocks when selected by pressing B, and the dark-colored fox (Destroyer) can destroy blocks when selected by pressing C. The arrow keys are used for movement, with the UP arrow used to jump, and the DOWN arrow used to crouch. The Space Bar is used to switch between characters per project specifications, ESC pauses the game, and in the demo, N can be used to instantly complete the current level.

The goal of Shape Quest is to have the hero, the blue square, collect all the coins in a level and then proceed to the unlocked exit. The hero follows the mouse cursor which allows for very precise movement. Within each level are various hazards that will damage the hero, colored in differing shades of red depending on how much damage they do, and a few potions, represented by green triangles, that can heal the hero by a small amount. There are also pitfalls, colored black, that will result in an instant game over if the hero goes too far into them and falls. The hero can pick up potions and coins by simply running into them. The player can press the P key or the ESC key to pause the game and bring up a menu with the options to skip the level, restart the level, or go back to the main menu. Pressing P or ESC again will resume the game.
